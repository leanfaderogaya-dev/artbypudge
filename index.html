About artbypudge

Lean is an illustrator/digital artist with a passion for all things creative. His works often explore life, nature, and nostalgia, brought to life through intricate line work and a cohesive color palette.

Inspired by music, film, and literature, he seeks to add a touch of whimsy to the everyday, turning the mundane into something magical.
